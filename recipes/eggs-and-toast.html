eggs and toast

a breakfast classic

a quick breakfast meal to hit those macros!

  • egg:6 to 8 eggs
  • spices:black pepper and salt
  • sauce:ketchup unless you're a monster that likes hot sauce
  • bread:two slice of whole wheat bread

cooking instructions

  1. cook and scramble the eggs
  2. toast two slices of bread
  3. pour eggs over bread
  4. add black pepper, salt, and ketchup then enjoy!
