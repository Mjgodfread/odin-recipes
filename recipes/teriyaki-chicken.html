Teriyaki Chicken

a protein packed meal

a simple dish that has become a staple in my household of fit meals.

ingredients

  • chicken: two chicken breasts
  • rice: two cups of nishiki brand white rice
  • seasonings-spices: garlic salt and sesame seeds
  • sauce: storebought teriyaki sauce, my preference is panda express' sauce
  • oil: extra virgin oilive oil

cooking instructions

  1. cook the white rice, steam to keep warm
  2. season the chicken in garlic salt and olive oil, grill until golden brown
  3. pour in teriyaki sauce and stir fry
  4. top chiken on a bed of rice and add sesame seeds
