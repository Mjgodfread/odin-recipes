lasagna

an easy to make dish for the family

Making lasagna can be hard, but with this recipe it doesn't have to be. Follow the instructions below to get started

ingredients

  • meat: italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • onion and garlic: one onion and two cloves of garlic
  • tomatoes:one crushed tomato, two cans of tomato sauce, two can of tomato paste
  • sugar:two tablespoons of sugar
  • spices:parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • lasagna noodles:any storebought noodles will do.
  • cheese:Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • egg:one large egg

cooking inctructions

  1. make the meat sauce
  2. cook the noodles
  3. make the ricotta mixture
  4. layer the lasagna
  5. cover with foil and bake
  6. let the lasagna cool before serving
